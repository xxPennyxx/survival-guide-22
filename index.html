Introduction

The Association of Students of Computer Science for Information Interchange (ASCII) is the technical association of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.
The association hosts various technical and social events planned and executed by students. The field of Computer Science is ever evolving and has varied applications and it is imperative that upcoming professionals have a clear picture of the latest trends and upcoming research avenues.

This event aims to develop a sense of enthusiasm among computer science students and give them a thirst of knowledge as they step into the marvellous world of computer science and take in all it has to offer.

With fun quizzes, activities and tools to get you going along your journey, we hope you enjoy the learning process and have fun along the way!
